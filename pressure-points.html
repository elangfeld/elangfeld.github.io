"YOU COULD BE MY PLATFORM."

In this voice memo, I was working on the bridge section of "Pressure Points." During the production process, I spliced up this exact voice memo and used it in the recording in place of a normal bridge because it felt more significant to me. It felt right to use a raw audio since the song is so vulnerable. It was summer, the windows were open, and birds were singing – it was such a peaceful moment. The birds ended up sampled in other places throughout the song as well.

I wrote "Pressure Points" about idolizing someone as the hope I was looking for through a struggle with mental health, and the fear of losing them. I felt like I was finally seen for my full potential, which gave me a glimpse of possibility when I could only see nothing before. The idea of "pressure points" felt so strangely accurate to me when I came up with it – it's not like someone is outright saving you, but more that they're just existing in the right places and the right ways, and it's really subtle, but that makes it all the more powerful.
