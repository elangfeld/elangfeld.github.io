When reality became too much, ELUNIA created an alternative existence. A singer-songwriter-producer executing all of her own visuals, the New Hampshire-based recording artist captures the endless meaning of human interaction as an immersive experience. Each track delves beneath the surface, with striking electronic textures, soaring melodies, and observant, probing lyrics.

Although trained in music from an early age, it was the discovery of transforming feelings into creative entities that inspired the emerging songsmith to chart a different path. The multi-instrumentalist began writing and producing incessantly, hiding away in school practice rooms as a coping mechanism for the isolation she faced in the world around her. Fragments of thought noted in the midst of heart-wrenching moments evolved into ethereal, liquid atmospheres. Co-producing with multi-million streamed producer JMAC (Haux, Luz, Matilda Mann, Rosie Carney), the result is a flood of wild self-expression channeled through cinematic soundscapes.

ELUNIA’s sophomore anthem “Soak,” with its lush, crystalline palette, evokes a post-concert euphoria after a lifelong struggle with mental health. The single was met with high praise, described by CLOUT as “the definition of a banger” and EARMILK as “[leaving] you hanging off each word.”
