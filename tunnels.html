"IT'S AN INSIDE JOKE WITH MYSELF."

When I wrote "Tunnels," it happened quickly one day in a single burst. The lyric "they're weaving tunnels round me" came to me while I was in the car, and then I wrote the rest of it as soon as I got home. I barely had to experiment before I landed on the right chords and melodies, which is rare for me. It was incredibly liberating to capture a feeling that I had never been able to put into words before.

"Tunnels" was probably the most painful track I wrote for the EP. An analogy dawned on me – it felt like I was standing on the ground, and there were all of these tunnels underneath my feet that I couldn’t see that other people were walking through, meeting in, laughing in, but I was always missing out. So many things were happening that I was never a part of, and so I would hear about them in snippets and feel too innocent, inexperienced, even inept. I pretended not to care, since I didn’t want to participate – but in reality, I cared so much it hurt.
