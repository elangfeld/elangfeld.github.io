"I WILL SAY WHAT YOU'RE AFRAID OF."

After years of writing down pieces of lyrics that never quite fit together, this was the first time I was able to create something cohesive for "The Cycle." The reworked lyrics came together simultaneously with the music. Later, in the process of production, I ended up slightly changing the chord progression from what you hear in this original voice memo to give it an even darker feel.

"The Cycle" was written and rewritten over quite a long period of time, and captures my experience with bullying over the years. I was psychologically manipulated to feel like everything I said and did was “wrong” and I wasn’t worthy of being included or even acknowledged, which made me self-conscious to the point of paranoia. This was an exhausting pattern that repeated over and over again in multiple situations in my life. As I got older, I began to realize a potential motive – I was always a bit too honest and would point out truths about others in a way that made them feel uncomfortably “seen” and afraid – so they were trying to establish power over me before I could over them.
